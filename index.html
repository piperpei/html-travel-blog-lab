Adventures in Fire Island

Visiting Fire Island in New York was an unforgettable experience.

Fire Island offers countless historic sites to go and explore. Being able to see the lighthouses up close was a breathtaking experience that I recommend everyone has the chance to see in their lifetime. Many other historic structures were surrounded by the beauty of nature.

Not only were there historical structures but a vast amount of plants and animals to see. The beaches were wonderful and the trails offered a peaceful escape in nature.
